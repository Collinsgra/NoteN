NoteNova Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 16, 2026

NoteNova ("we", "our", or "us") is a privacy-first note-taking mobile application developed by Koch Tech.

1. Information We Collect

NoteNova is designed as an offline-first application. We do not collect or store your personal notes, content, or writings on any server.

We may collect the following limited, non-sensitive information:

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and language preference.
  • App Usage Data: Anonymous usage statistics to improve the app.
  • Advertising Identifiers: Google Advertising ID (for showing relevant ads).

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, location, or any personal notes unless you voluntarily provide them through support.

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Improve app performance and stability
  • Provide and optimize features
  • Display personalized advertisements via Google AdMob
  • Analyze anonymous usage trends

3. Advertising

We use Google AdMob to show ads in the free version of the app.

AdMob may collect and process certain data such as device identifiers, IP address, and ad interaction data.

You can learn more about Google’s advertising practices here: Google Advertising Policies

Premium users who purchase the "Remove Ads" upgrade will no longer see advertisements.

4. In-App Purchases

NoteNova offers a one-time purchase to remove ads permanently. All payments are securely processed by Google Play Billing. We do not store or have access to your payment information.

5. Data Storage and Sync

All your notes, documents, and attachments are stored locally on your device. We do not upload, backup, or sync your notes to any cloud server.

Exports (PDF, Word, Text) are saved directly to your device's Downloads folder.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the data stored locally on your device. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We encourage you to keep your device secure with a screen lock.

7. Children’s Privacy

NoteNova is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us so we can delete it.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here with an updated effective date. We recommend reviewing this page periodically.
